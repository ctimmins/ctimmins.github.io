One Probe per Acre

Each root probe monitors air temperature, soil temperature, humidity, and soil moisture. The probes are strategically placed around a field at a density of one per acre to give a 360 degree view of all your fields.

Easy and Powerful

root probes automatically connect and begin transmitting data to our web platform. With our unique mesh network, we are able to reach all corners of your fields, giving you the freedom to monitor what you want.

Reliability

We have created a sensor that is durable, accurate, and low-cost. This allows farmers to have as many probes as they need to monitor each individual field without breaking the bank.
