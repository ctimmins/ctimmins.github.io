About Me



Who am I?

I am a rising Electrical Engineer from UC Davis graduating in March 2015. Without a doubt, these last four years have been the most difficult times I have had in terms of work and personal growth. Of course, with great difficulty comes great reward. I always look for learning opportunities in any task or interaction and am continuously trying to improve upon myself. I am driven by passion and am rewarded by accomplisments.

What I do for fun

Yes, I deal with binary a lot, but I like to think I'm not your average engineer. Outside of school, I enjoy hiking and exploring the limitless beauty that is nature. I also thoroughly enjoy all kinds of music from a Chopin masterpiece to ear-bleeding sets from Zed's Dead. My passion for music spawns immense creativity and I am always looking for new ways to consume it.

What I am looking for

Although I come from a hardware background, I have focused on software and web development as of late. Seeing an application come together from both ends of the stack is very intriquing and I am only beginning to realize the endless complexities between the user, software, and hardware. Although I appreciate the importance of user experience and design, my skills and interests would best be utilized in a backend role. My diverse background has helped me in developing robust software and will help me become a successful engineer working on the backend of the stack.
